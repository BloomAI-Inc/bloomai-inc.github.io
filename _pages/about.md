Bloom AI provides a **Modern Intelligence Layer** designed to help organizations overcome decision-making delays through AI-powered insights. We empower investment managers, insurance companies, banks, and Fortune 1000 enterprises to make faster, more informed decisions.

## Our Platform

We deliver three core solutions:

**SynthBI** - AI-powered automated analytical summaries and intelligent nudges personalized to your work context, enabling teams to uncover actionable insights instantly.

**DYSTL** - Transforms unstructured data into GenAI-powered decision intelligence, unlocking insights hidden in your data.

**AI Services** - Subscription-based GenAI co-pilots and analytical APIs that integrate seamlessly into your existing workflows.
